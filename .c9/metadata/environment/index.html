About

\n

I am 20 years old and currently enrolled in Code Institute's Software Development course. I have never coded before, but my dad has been programming for as long as I can remember so I guess it was just a matter of time before I decided\n to take a look at it myself. It's quite a new shift for me as I have been bodybuilding and into fitness for over half a decade, but I love learning new things and to challenge myself.\n I have actually competed in Men's Physique 3 times before and have included a couple pictures from my last competition in August 2019.

\n

I was born in Switzerland and lived there for 2 years before my family moved back to Norway, where I was raised (mostly). 6th grade in middle school my family decided to move in South Africa so we decided to move to Cape Town, South\n Africa where I went to American International School of Cape Town for a year before we went back home. After completing Norwegian highschool, I felt like I wanted to do something with my life as well as being tired and fed up of\n the Norwegian school system so I ended up going to Marine Military Academy in Harlingen, Texas for my Junior year.

\n

I enjoy solving challenges and coming up with different solutions. I especially enjoy the feeling I get when I complete a challenge without having to look it up or research it and just using the knowledge I have been taught. There are\n numerous things that I have already decided on that I want to learn more about, but it'll have to wait until I finish Code Institute's Software Development program first.
